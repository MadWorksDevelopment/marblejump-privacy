Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2025

Marble Jump (“the App”) is developed by MadWorks Development (“we”, “us”, or “our”). This privacy policy explains how your data is collected and used when using the App.

1. Data We Collect

  • Game Center: We use Apple Game Center to store your leaderboard scores and achievements.
  • User Defaults & iCloud: We store your in-game progress, settings, and rewards using Apple’s local storage and iCloud sync to enable continuity across devices.
  • AdMob (Google Ads): We use AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device information, advertising ID, IP address, and usage data. You can review Google's policy here.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide in-game functionality such as saving your progress, showing ads, and enabling leaderboards.

3. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal data, contact us to have it removed.
